Key insights

🔥 Most Important Factor: Physical activity (steps) is the strongest predictor of weight change at 41.8% feature importance. Increasing daily steps significantly impacts weight loss.

📊 Strong Model Fit: With R² = 0.9912, the Random Forest model explains 99.12% of variance in weight change, indicating excellent predictive power.

⚖️ Calorie-Activity Balance: While calories (26.3%) drive weight gain, physical activity (41.8%) is even more influential, suggesting that exercise intensity may outweigh dietary restrictions.

😴 Sleep & Fasting Matter: Fasting hours (24.6%) and sleep (3.0%) have complementary effects, both contributing to weight loss when increased.

📱 Screen Time Impact: Sedentary behavior (screen time) shows positive correlation with weight gain but has lower feature importance (4.2%), likely because it's captured by the activity level.
